Fettuccine Alfredo

Fettuccine Alfredo is arguably one of the most delicious pasta dishes out there. Made with just 7 simple ingredients, this easy recipe is sure to wow any dinner guests. Due to the simple steps, it also makes for a great evening activity. Grab a bottle of Pinot Noir, turn up some soothing jazz tunes, and enjoy bonding with friends over your newfound Michelin-level skills.

Ingredients

  • 2 - 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 50g of unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspons of all-purpose flour
  • A few handfuls of fresh baby spinach
  • A pinch of salt
  • 100g of heavy cream
  • Optional: cooked protein (shrimp, chicken, tofu)
  • A pack of Fettuccine pasta

Steps:

  1. Fry the garlic in the butter.
  2. Add the flour and continue to stir, allowing the garlic and flour to fry in the butter.
  3. Add the spinach and stir.
  4. Sprinkle in a pinch of salt and mix in the heavy cream, allowing the mixture to simmer.
  5. Gauge the consistency of the cream; if it's too thick add some water, if it's too thin allow it to cook and evaporate.
  6. Optionally, you can add a protein in - shrimp, chicken, tofu, any of these would make great additions.
